Built for serious email campaigns

Every feature designed around the needs of high-volume, reliable email delivery.

schedule_send

Persistent Scheduling

Emails are stored in a persistent database, not just in-memory queues. Survive server restarts without losing a single scheduled email.

speed

Rate Limiting

Built-in per-user hourly rate limits prevent spam and respect SMTP provider quotas. Automatically reschedules overflow to the next window.

autorenew

Automatic Retries

Failed emails are automatically retried up to 3 times with exponential backoff. Track every attempt in a complete audit trail.

upload_file

CSV Bulk Upload

Upload a CSV with thousands of recipient emails and schedule them all in one campaign. Our parser validates every email address automatically.

analytics

Real-time Campaign Tracking

Monitor sent, failed, and pending emails in real time. View campaign progress with beautiful dashboards and detailed per-email status.

fingerprint

Idempotent Delivery

Unique idempotency keys prevent duplicate sends even during system restarts. Every email is sent exactly once — guaranteed.

How Velo processes your campaigns

An 8-table database architecture designed for reliability, auditability, and scale.

1
person

User Authentication

Secure user accounts with OAuth support. Each user gets isolated campaigns, rate limits, and SMTP configurations.

2
campaign

Campaign Creation

Define subject, body, recipients via CSV, and scheduling. Denormalized counters track sent/failed/pending in real time.

3
email

Individual Email Tracking

Every email gets its own record with scheduled_time, status lifecycle (scheduled → queued → sent/failed), and attempt tracking.

4
timer

Rate Limit Enforcement

Fixed hourly windows with atomic counters ensure users never exceed their SMTP provider's limits. Overflow auto-reschedules.

5
receipt_long

Complete Audit Trail

Every send attempt is logged with SMTP response codes, duration, and error details. Supports debugging and compliance.

6
dns

Multi-SMTP Support

Configure multiple sender accounts with encrypted credentials. Round-robin and failover strategies for reliability.
